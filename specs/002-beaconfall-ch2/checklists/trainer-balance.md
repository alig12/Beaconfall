# Chapter 2 Trainer Balance Checklist

**Feature**: Chapter 2 - The Tide Beneath Brassfall  
**Purpose**: Verify the chapter's battles fit the hybrid trainer budget and support the intended difficulty curve.

## Core Roster

- [x] The chapter stays within the hybrid trainer budget.
- [x] Optional mini-boss content is deferred rather than forcing an oversized roster.
- [x] Route battles are fewer and lighter than the dungeon and gym battles.
- [x] The dungeon battles support the puzzle without overshadowing it.
- [x] The rival battle acts as a checkpoint rather than a spike wall.
- [x] The gym leader feels like a chapter finale without requiring a new battle system.

## Difficulty Curve

- [x] Saltwind Coast is easier than Floodgate Tunnels.
- [x] Floodgate Tunnels is harder than the opening route but still fair.
- [x] Astral Tide Gym is the highest-pressure section of the chapter.
- [x] The chapter feels like a step up from Chapter 1 while still being fair on first attempts.

## Notes

- Keep the roster compact enough to avoid trainer-ID pressure.
- Trim optional combat before trimming the chapter's core story battles.
