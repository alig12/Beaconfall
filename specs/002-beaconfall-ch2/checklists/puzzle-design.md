# Chapter 2 Puzzle Design Checklist

**Feature**: Chapter 2 - The Tide Beneath Brassfall  
**Purpose**: Verify the Floodgate and gym water puzzles are readable, recoverable, and fun.

## Floodgate Tunnels

- [x] The water states are visually distinct enough that the player can tell when the dungeon changed.
- [x] Every lever choice leaves at least one recoverable path.
- [x] Incorrect lever choices never trap the player in a soft-lock.
- [x] Hint text or NPC guidance points the player toward the intended valve order.
- [x] The puzzle changes path access, not just scenery, so the mechanic feels meaningful.

## Astral Tide Gym

- [x] The gym reuses the water-state idea in a shorter, cleaner layout than Floodgate.
- [x] The gym puzzle is readable without repeating the full dungeon complexity.
- [x] The leader room stays gated until the puzzle is solved.
- [x] The final puzzle step feels like a payoff, not a second copy of the dungeon.

## Notes

- Floodgate is the chapter's main puzzle space.
- The gym should feel like a sharper version of the same language, not a longer version.
